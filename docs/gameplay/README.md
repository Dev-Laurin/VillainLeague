# Gameplay Documentation

This directory contains player-focused documentation about game mechanics and how to play Villain League.

## Purpose

These guides explain gameplay mechanics from a **player's perspective**, helping players understand:
- How game systems work
- Combat mechanics and strategies
- Character stats and abilities
- Items and equipment
- Win conditions and objectives

## Content Guidelines

When writing gameplay documentation:

### Focus on the Player Experience
- Write in second person ("You can...")
- Explain what players see and experience
- Use clear, non-technical language
- Include strategic tips and advice

### Use Examples and Scenarios
- Show concrete examples of mechanics
- Describe actual gameplay situations
- Use "what if" scenarios to clarify
- Include screenshots when helpful

### Structure Information Clearly
- Use headings and subheadings
- Create bulleted lists for options
- Add "Quick Reference" sections
- Include a table of contents for long guides

## Example Topics

- **Combat Guide**: How battles work, turn order, actions
- **Character Stats Guide**: What each stat does, how to build characters
- **Special Abilities Guide**: All special moves and when to use them
- **Status Effects Guide**: All status effects and their impacts
- **Strategy Guide**: Advanced tactics and team compositions
- **Items Guide**: All items, their effects, and usage

## Format Example

```markdown
## Blocking in Combat

### What Is Blocking?
Blocking is a defensive action that reduces incoming damage by 50% until your next turn.

### When to Use It
- Enemy is about to use a special attack
- Your HP is low and you need to survive
- Waiting for the right moment to counter-attack

### How to Block
1. Click the "Defend" button during your turn
2. Your character enters a defensive stance
3. All damage received is reduced until your next action

### Pro Tips
- Block before powerful enemy attacks
- Use when outnumbered to buy time
- Combine with healing for maximum survivability
```
